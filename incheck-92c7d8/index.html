Compliance & Regulatory Triggers

New regulations force healthcare orgs to reassess screening partners

Virginia Clean Slate Law (July 2026)

Record-sealing changes what shows in background checks. Healthcare employers need partners who understand new visibility rules.

State Regulation

Washington Fair Chance Act (July 2026)

Employers with 15+ employees cannot request criminal history until after conditional offer. Timing and process changes required.

State Regulation

OIG Exclusion List Monthly Checks

Healthcare providers must check LEIE at least monthly for all employees, contractors, and vendors. Non-compliance = civil monetary penalties.

Federal Requirement

CMS Network Adequacy 2026

Medicaid certification requires stricter compliance. Health plans face federal scrutiny on credentialing and screening processes.

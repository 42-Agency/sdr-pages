Platform-Wide Industry Problems

Common complaints across digital member engagement vendors

01 Low Adoption Rates

"Only one-third of patients offered a portal ever log in, and 80% of users stop using health apps within 30 days."

02 Fragmented Point Solutions

"Care teams spend more time toggling between systems than engaging with members. Poor interoperability impedes whole-person care."

03 Data Silos

"Legacy CAPS cause data sharing bottlenecks. Members receive conflicting or unclear information across touchpoints."

04 Security Vulnerabilities

"Virgin Pulse/Welltok data breach exposed 8M+ individuals' SSNs, Medicare IDs, and health information via MOVEit vulnerability."
